### Uncertainty Assessment Task
For EACH tweet, determine:

1. **Plausibility (Likert 1–5)**
   To what extent does this tweet plausibly represent a real-world situational
   development during this event, given:
   - the PAGER summary
   - known limitations of PAGER
   - the specificity, tone, and credibility of the tweet content
   - the timing of the tweet relative to the event

   - 5 = highly plausible and credible
   - 3 = uncertain or ambiguous
   - 1 = implausible or unlikely to reflect real conditions

   A tweet may be plausible even if it contradicts PAGER.

2. **Confidence (0–100)**
   How confident are you in your plausibility judgment?

3. **Discrepancy assessment**
   If the tweet appears weakly plausible or implausible, decide why:

   - "none" → no clear discrepancy

4. **Rationale**
   Briefly explain your reasoning, explicitly referencing:

   - its SA category/categories
   - the timing of the tweet
   - how it relates to or diverges from the PAGER summary
   - why it may still be plausible (or not)
